My Story

I didn’t get into real estate because it was easy or flashy. I got into it because I learned early how important the right guidance really is.

At 21, I purchased my first investment property. The transaction didn’t go as planned. Unexpected issues surfaced, details were missed, and I quickly realized how costly it can be when you don’t fully understand what’s happening behind the scenes. That experience shaped how I approach real estate today.

It pushed me to build a business centered on trust, clear communication, and education, so clients always know where they stand and why decisions are being made. I believe real estate should feel straightforward—not confusing or rushed.

Today, I work with buyers, sellers, and investors throughout central Indiana, helping them make confident, informed decisions backed by real numbers and honest expectations. I don’t focus on just getting deals done. I focus on representing people the right way, protecting their interests, and making sure there are no surprises along the way.
